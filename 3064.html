Persuasion (3064)

Beth Waggenspack
Department of Communication
Director of Graduate Studies, Associate Professor

Overview

In this class we discuss concepts that surround the art of persuasion, how to get the message you are presenting across to different audience types, how to see through persuasive devices and be critical thinkers, and where certain persuasive devices will often times be utilized and how to pick them out.
