Travel and Tourism Management (2454)

Nancy McGehee
Department of Hospitality and Tourism Management
Department Head, Professor

Overview

In this class we talk about the global and local impacts of tourism. We read about the everchanging and growing market of tourism, as well as explore both the positive and negative effects it can have within certain areas. Seeing as this class is online our main grade consists of a term project that involves us planning out a vacation under very specific parameters.
