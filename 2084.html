Media and Society (2084)

Jenn MacKay
Department of Communication
Associate Professor

Overview

In this class we look at how media shapes society and discuss the impacts it can have. We make sure to look at a variety of different media types we are exposed to daily, whether that be newspapers, film, social networks, or video games, and how they affect a wide range of audiences. The class is explores the evolution of media, trends related to media, intercultural communication, the influence of new technology on society, and so much more.
